## 📱 Step-by-Step Setup

### 1️⃣ **Create Your Account**
1. Go to: `https://bundles-store.vercel.app`
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Password
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email for verification link
6. Login with your new account

### 2️⃣ **Create Your Store**
1. After login, go to **Dashboard**
2. Click **"Create My Store"**
3. Fill in store information:
   - Store name (e.g., "Quick Data Hub")
   - Store description
   - Contact information
4. Click **"Create Store"**
5. Wait for admin approval (usually within 24 hours)

### 3️⃣ **Set Your Prices**
1. Once approved, go to **"Pricing"** in your dashboard
2. You'll see all data bundles organized by network:
   - **MTN** (Yellow)
   - **AirtelTigo** (Red) 
   - **Telecel** (Blue)
3. **Set Custom Prices:**
   - Click **"Edit"** next to any package
   - Enter your desired selling price
   - Click **"Save Changes"**
4. **Quick Options:**
   - **"Apply Margin to All"** - Add percentage to all prices
   - **"Reset to Default"** - Go back to original prices

### 4️⃣ **Get Your Store Link**
1. In your dashboard, go to **"My Store"**
2. Copy your unique store link:
   ```
   https://bundles-store.vercel.app/store/[your-store-name]
   ```
3. **Test it:** Visit your link to see how customers will see your store

### 5️⃣ **Start Selling & Earning**
1. **Share your store link** with customers via:
   - WhatsApp
   - Social media (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter)
   - Business cards
   - Word of mouth
2. **Customers buy directly** from your store link
3. **You earn commission** on every sale
4. **Track earnings** in your dashboard

## 💰 How You Earn Money

### Commission Structure
- **Every sale** = Commission based on your profit margin
- **Higher prices** = Higher commission per sale
- **No limits** on earnings

### Example Earnings
```
Package: 1GB MTN Data
Base Cost: GHS 5.00
Your Price: GHS 7.00
Your Profit: GHS 2.00 per sale
```

### Withdraw Your Earnings
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **"Wallet"**
2. Click **"Transfer to Wallet"**
3. Enter amount to transfer
4. Funds move to your main wallet
5. Withdraw to bank account anytime
